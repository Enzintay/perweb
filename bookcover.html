Book Cover
The concept for the book cover began with trying to keep with the original art style of the book with only black and white sketch art.
However, the aim of the project was to redesign with the goal in mind of rebranding and appealing to the audience of our time.
As such, in order to maintain a semblance of the original art style whilst appealing to a newer audience, I kept the sketch-like
outlines of the original but added color and gradients to give more life to the drawings on the cover.
